Huizhong Chen is now an Imaging Algorithms and Software Engineer with Google, Mountain View, California. In September 2015, he graduated with a Ph.D degree from the Electrical Engineering Department, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, where he worked in the Image, Video and Multimedia Systems group led by Prof. Bernd Girod. Previously, Huizhong received the B.A. and M.Eng. degrees with First Class honor from the Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge, UK, in 2008 and 2009 respectively. Huizhong is a Fellow of the Brown Institute of Media Innovation, and a Kodak Fellow.

Huizhong's primary research area is computer vision, but he is interested in solving all sorts of data analysis problems. During his Ph.D studies, he worked on a wide range of projects, including text detection/recognition, visual attributes learning, facial processing, and multi-modal information retrieval.
